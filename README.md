#📄 ClimateShield: AI-Powered Income Safety for Gig Workers

##🧠 Problem

Delivery partners lose daily income due to uncontrollable disruptions like extreme weather and pollution, with no existing protection mechanism for such losses.

##💡 Solution

ClimateShield is a parametric, AI-driven micro-insurance platform that automatically compensates delivery workers for income loss based on real-time environmental triggers — with zero manual claims.

##🔄 How It Works

Worker enrolls and pays a weekly premium
AI evaluates location-based risk
System monitors live environmental data
Trigger event → auto claim → instant payout

##💰 Weekly Pricing

Dynamic pricing (₹40–₹75/week) based on:
Location risk
Historical disruption patterns

##⚡ Smart Triggers

Rainfall > 50 mm
Temperature > 40°C
AQI > 300

##🤖 AI Edge

Predictive risk modeling
Dynamic premium adjustment
Fraud/anomaly detection

##📱 Platform & Tech

Mobile-first | Flutter/React | Node.js/Python | MongoDB | Weather APIs

##🚫 Scope

Strictly covers income loss only (no health, accident, or asset claims).

🚀 Impact

Transforms insurance into a real-time financial safety net for gig workers — simple, automated, and scalable.
